SOFTWARE ASSET MANAGEMENT

In today’s fast-paced, ever-changing business environment, organisations are faced with a dilemma: how to embrace new technologies, manage their complexities effectively and get the best out of them in order to gain significant competitive advantage while reducing cost. And, that’s where Software Asset Management (SAM) comes in. Simply put, SAM is a service for utilizing, managing, controlling and safeguarding your organisation’s software assets. An effective SAM solution ensures that your software assets remain of utmost value to your organisation throughout their lifecycles. Position Your IT Assets and Your Organization for Success Using TORF Technologies SAM Services At TORF Technologies, we integrate the global SAM Optimization Model (SOM) with the Industrial Organization (IO) model to provide your organisation with a comprehensive framework where people, policies, processes and tools work together to:

  • Reduce/Optimize IT asset costs (purchase and utilization phases)
    Optimize investments by redeploying underused IT assets
    Increase IT efficiency by adopting faster, simpler and better business processes
We provide you this service through detailed discovery, management and utilization analytics of all your IT resources. We then proffer a top-quality and cost-effective SAM solution that is professionally tailored to meet the business needs of your organization. Take Advantage of Our Comprehensive SAM Service to Gain Competitive Leverage and Save Costs The TORF Technologies SAM service spans the entire lifecycle of your IT resources, i.e., hardware and software. It covers:
  • Technology and License Purchase
    IT Asset/Software Deployment Management
    IT Asset/Software Management and Maintenance
    IT Asset/Software Utilization
    IT Asset/Software Disposal
    Compliance: Management of Legal Risks
Contact us now to help you acquire, manage, control and utilize your IT assets in the best possible way to meet your company’s business needs today.
