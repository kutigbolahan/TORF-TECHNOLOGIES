TORF LEARNING APPLICATION

TORF Learning is an educational solution developed by TORF Technologies; and aimed at improving the Mathematics performance of secondary school students in the country. Textbooks and other resources used on the platform are consistent with the approved curriculums; while simultaneously maintaining an international perspective to broaden the exposure of our students.
